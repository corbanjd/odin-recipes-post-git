Lasagna

Description

Brenda's lasagna is a real show stopper. Let me tell you! Ever since we made this, we can't stop getting compliments!

If you're looking for love, then look no further. People will be clawing at each other just to talk to you once you know how to make this dish!

Ingredients For Recipe

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Add Lasgna Noodles to cheese
  2. Pre-heat oven to 450
  3. Bake for 45 minutes
